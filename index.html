About Us

Founded as an early-stage, bootstrapped startup, Radhakriti Tech Solution is currently in stealth mode, dedicated to pioneering the next generation of Artificial Intelligence. We specialize in cutting-edge Generative AI (GenAI) and are actively engaged in research and development for Strong Artificial Intelligence.

Our vision is to become a globally recognized leader in AI-driven solutions. We aim to empower businesses and organizations with intelligent automation and data-driven innovation, driving transformative growth and unparalleled efficiency.

Our mission is to conceptualize, develop, and deploy groundbreaking intelligent systems. These systems are designed to transcend current GenAI capabilities, enabling us to unlock entirely new frontiers of possibility for our clients. The company is led by its initial Co-founder, an experienced software architect and product leader focused on creating and deploying advanced AI applications.

Our Advanced Services

Custom Generative AI Application Development

We engineer bespoke Generative AI solutions that extend far beyond standard models. These are highly specialized, performant, and deeply integrated AI systems designed to achieve outcomes conventional tools cannot.

Pioneering Futuristic & Strong AI Concepts

We commit to fundamental R&D, integrating experimental and advanced AI models that exhibit characteristics approaching Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). This involves deep dives into meta-learning, causal inference, and highly autonomous systems.

Intelligent Agent Development & Orchestration

We construct sophisticated "Agentic AI" solutions – autonomous entities capable of performing multi-step tasks, engaging in long-term planning, dynamic tool use, and complex decision-making.

AI Data Strategy & Management

Our expertise encompasses architecting sophisticated data pipelines, managing vast arrays of embeddings, and building advanced vector stores to enable dynamic knowledge graphs and contextual reasoning engines.

Full-Stack AI Deployment & MLOps

We establish cutting-edge MLOps practices for deploying, monitoring, and maintaining entire autonomous AI systems in production, including automated model discovery and self-healing deployment pipelines.
